Our website was created to help everyone keep track of their budgets, no matter how many. Budget plans are easy to use and create. Budget plans are automatically deleted when there are no expenses for more than 7 days.
It's design is clear and very easy to understand. Do you want to use our Budget Buck$, Follow the steps:


Step 1

Click the "Sign Up Now" button at the top right of the webpage.

Step 2

Fill out the neccessary fields required to successfully create your account.

Step 3

You will now be redirected to a page that allows you to start a budget plan.
Click New Budget Plan.

Step 4

You will now be prompt to add your expected budget, expenses and amount spent.

Step 5

Your total budget amount left after added expenses and total transactions made
will be displayed.

Step 6

Close the "Create New Budget Plan" tab to view or create new budget plans.
